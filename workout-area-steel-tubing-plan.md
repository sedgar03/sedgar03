# Workout Area Steel Tubing Plan

## Goal
Source 3x3" 11-gauge (.120" wall) steel square tubing in 10–12 foot lengths with 5/8" holes drilled in a Westside hole spacing pattern, to build out a custom workout area compatible with Rogue Monster Lite accessories.

---

## 1. Steel Tubing Specs (Matching Rogue SML-2)

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Profile** | 3" x 3" square tube |
| **Wall Thickness** | 11 gauge / 0.120" (1/8") |
| **Steel Grade** | ASTM A500 Grade B (structural) or A513 (mechanical) |
| **Hole Diameter** | 5/8" laser-cut or drilled |
| **Hole Spacing** | Westside pattern (see below) |
| **Upright Length** | 10 ft or 12 ft |
| **Base Tubes** | 2x3" 11-gauge (if building bases) |
| **Weight** | ~5.59 lbs/ft for 3x3 11ga |

### Westside Hole Spacing Pattern
- **Bottom zone (holes 1–11):** 2" spacing between holes
- **Bench press zone (holes 11–32):** 1" spacing (precision zone)
- **Top zone (holes 32–52):** 2" spacing between holes
- Holes centered on one face of the tube, 5/8" diameter
- For a full rack, drill matching holes on opposing faces

This gives you fine adjustment in the critical bench/rack pull range while keeping the total hole count manageable.

| Coating | Cost Estimate | Durability | Best For | Notes |
|---------|--------------|------------|----------|-------|

### Recommendation
- **Indoor garage gym:** Professional powder coat in black or color of choice (~$400–600 for 6–8 uprights)
- **Outdoor setup:** Hot-dip galvanized (~$400–800 depending on weight)
- **Budget indoor:** Red oxide primer + Rustoleum spray paint (~$50–80 total)

## 5. Hole Drilling Plan

### Option A: DIY Drilling
- **Tool:** Drill press strongly recommended (floor-standing, not benchtop)
- **Bit:** 5/8" annular cutter or stepped approach (1/4" pilot → 5/8" finish)
- **Jig:** Build a drilling jig that indexes off the previous hole for consistent 1" and 2" spacing
- **Cutting fluid:** Use metal cutting oil generously
- **Time estimate:** ~2–4 hours per upright (50+ holes per tube)
- **Cost:** $50–150 for bits/cutters + jig materials

### Option B: CNC Laser or Plasma Cutting (Local Fab Shop)
- Many metal fabrication shops have CNC plasma or laser tables
- Provide a DXF/DWG drawing with hole positions
- Expect $50–150 per upright for hole cutting
- Cleanest holes, most consistent results

### Option C: Buy Pre-Drilled
- [GetRXd Builder Tubes](https://www.getrxd.com/3-x-3-builder-rig-upright-tubes.html) come pre-drilled
- Higher cost per tube but zero fabrication work
- Guaranteed compatibility with their accessories

### Recommendation
If you have access to a drill press, Option A is the most cost-effective. If not, get quotes from a local fab shop (Option B) — it's worth it for clean, consistent holes.

## 6. Hardware

For Rogue Monster Lite compatible setups:

| Item | Spec | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| J-cups / J-hooks | Monster Lite compatible (3x3, 5/8" pin) | Rogue?"monster lite" or third-party (Titan, Rep) |
| Safety pins | 5/8" pin with pipe sleeve | Pin/pipe design for strength |
| Bolts (assembly) | SAE Grade 5, 5/8" diameter | For connecting uprights to bases |
| Pull-up bar | 1.25" OD steel pipe or fat bar | Weld or bolt between uprights |

> **Tip on safety:** For heavy rack pulls or drops, consider upgrading to 1" hole diameter with 1" pins, or use the pin/pipe safety system (small pin through the upright, larger pipe resting on the pins). The 5/8" pins alone can bend under extreme loads.

## 7. Action Plan / Next Steps

1. **Determine layout** — How many uprights do you need? (4 for a full rack, 2 for a squat stand, 6+ for a rig)
2. **Decide on length** — 10 ft gives ~8 ft usable height after base attachment; 12 ft allows for overhead pressing and muscle-up bar height
3. **Get quotes** — Contact 2–3 suppliers above for current pricing on your quantity
4. **Check local** — Visit a local steel yard for potentially better pricing and no shipping cost
5. **Decide coating** — Indoor (powder coat) vs outdoor (galvanized)
6. **Plan hole drilling** — DIY with jig, or get quotes from a local fab shop
7. **Order accessories** — J-cups, safeties, pull-up bar (Monster Lite compatible)
8. **Assembly hardware** — Grade 5 bolts, gusset plates if needed
